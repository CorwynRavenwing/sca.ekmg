Jewelry / Gems / Metalwork Terms & Definitions

The following are definitions of terms used for jewelry, gems, and metalwork. Some are technical terms from the jewelry and metalwork trades; some are names from other cultures and languages. Unless otherwise stated, the terms come from Carowyn and her Library of Doom. If you have a suggestion of a term that should be added to this page, whether or not you know the definition yourself, please contact the Guildmistress.
