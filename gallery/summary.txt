This album is full of pictures of work done by the members of the East Kingdom Metalsmiths' Guild, as well as pictures of the members themselves.  It is part of the <a href="http://ekmg.eastkingdom.org" target="_blank">EK Metalsmiths Guild</a> website. To contribute artwork or to request the removal of a picture, please contact the Webminister at <a href="mailto:webminister@ekmg.eastkingdom.org">webminister@ekmg.eastkingdom.org</a>.
