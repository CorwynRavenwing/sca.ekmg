A Maker's Mark is a small image that a jeweler, blacksmith, or other metalworker uses to identify his or her works. It is usually painted or stamped onto the piece somewhere inobtrusive, in the same way that a painter would use their signature. It is often either a symbol important to the user, or one that refers to part of the artist's name.

If you are a member of the Metalsmiths' Guild and would like your maker's mark to be posted here, please contact the Webminister at webminister@ekmg.eastkingdom.org, who will add you to the roster.
