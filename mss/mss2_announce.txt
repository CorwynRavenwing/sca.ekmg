Metal Symposium
**Updated Aug. 28, 2003**
Barony of Thescorre (Rochester, NY)
October 24 - 26

Come one and all for a weekend of Metal working!!! The event will be held
at the Masonic Camp 8039 Lake Rd. Sodus point, NY. The site opens at 5:00
PM Friday, October 24th and will close at Noon on Sunday, October 26th.

This event is open to all metal workers or inspiring metal workers. Metal
tipped shoes/ boots are highly recommended. This event is not suitable for
young children due to safety reasons (Not only are there forges, but a
cliff to Lake Ontario). Site is not handicapped accessible. There is a cap
of 80 people for this event, including teachers. Please send in your
reservation as soon as possible to reserve your spot!

Class details can be found under the event announcement in the Calendar
section of the Barony of Thescorre web page (www.thescorre.org). Full class
details may not be available until September 26th. People
interested in teaching classes please contact Lady Lavena Knappe
(jtucker12@...).

Friday evening enjoy some tasty treats as a travelers repass will be
presented. A delightful feast will be prepared Saturday evening by the
Baroness of Thescorre (Baroness Peregrine, baroness@...).
Please send any dietary concerns by September 30th. Let's not forget
a filling breakfast Saturday and Sunday mornings in addition, lunch and
snacks on Saturday!

This is a wet site with showers & restrooms. There is cabin space
available with 38 bunks (first come first served) as well as plenty floor
space (space heaters encouraged). Camping is welcome.

Fees for the event are as follows:

Lodging and food for weekend: $20 per person due September 30th to reserve
spot (Non-member $3.00 additional). See additional Class Fee Schedule.

Saturday Daytripper fee: $5 per person, includes Saturday lunch and
snacks, due October 10th to reserve spot (non-member $3.00 additional).
Feast available for an extra $5.00 per person. See additional Class Fee
Schedule.

Class Fee Schedule: Classes are extra, based on what you sign up for. You may
send in your
reservation before signing up for classes. However, all reservations and
classes must be paid on or before October 10th (supplies needed for
classes must be purchased in advance). Additional tools needed for the
class will be listed by the instructors. Class fees can be found under the
event announcement in the Calendar section of the Barony of Thescorre web
page (www.thescorre.org).

Please direct questions to Lady Lavena Knappe (jtucker12@...).

Checks should be made out to "SCA Inc. ? Barony of Thescorre". Please send
reservations to Eldjarn (Dan Clement, 6 Cobblestone Crossing, Penfield,
New York 14526).

The autocrats for this event are: Lady Lavena and Yoseph White
(yospehwhite@...).
