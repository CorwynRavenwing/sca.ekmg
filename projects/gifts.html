Gifts Made for Other People

This page documents gifts made by one or more Guild members, in the name of the guild, to be given to someone as a gift, usually but not always Their Majesties.

Hand Mirrors

 This project was performed for Their Majesties Kelson II & Geneviere II. They were made of wood, with a space carved out for a mirror (modern silvered glass rather than period polished silver: chosen for utility, for cost, and for speed.) Appropriate images, including the personal devices of His and Her Majesty, were wood-burned into the face and back of the mirror frame. They were presented in court at "Alles Faire in Love and War", AS XL.
