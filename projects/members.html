Projects by Guild Members

This page documents projects by one or more Guild members, but undertaken personally, rather than being done in the name of the Guild.

If you have done a project of this sort and would like to submit photos of it for this page, please contact the Webminister.

Artist: Kat

Project: Pelican medallion for Master Hawk

This is the Pelican medallion given at Mudthaw to Master Hawk. In homage to all the service he has provided as a Chirurgeon, one of the blood drops that the pelican is feeding her young, is actually the fleame-in-a-blood-drop symbol of the Chirurgeonate.

Artist: Carowyn Silveroak

Project: East Kingdom Arts & Sciences Tokens

These are tokens for the East Kingdom Mistress of Arts & Sciences to hand out at Arts and Sciences exhibitions and competitions. They were cast from pewter in a hand-carved soapstone mold. Corwyn and Carowyn both worked on this project.

Artist: Lillian Lyttle

Project: Gem Trees

These gem trees are made of wire and precious or semi-precious gems. Some are designed to stand on their own, and some are wired to a frame to be hung in the air to catch the light.
