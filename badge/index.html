(BACK)

We have begun the process of choosing a badge to represent our Guild. It will replace the blue-text-on-white link in the upper right of this page, as well as be used by Guild members to signify that someone is a member of, or that something is owned by, the Guild.

We are also seeking a motto for the guild, not necessarily in English, that signifies who we are and what we do.

Several people have made suggestions, which are presented here in no particular order.

If you are a member of the Metalsmiths' Guild and would like your maker's mark to be posted here, please contact the Guildmistress at guildmistress@ekmg.eastkingdom.org, who will add you to the roster.

(If you are not a member of the Guild but you would like to be, please contact the Guildmistress saying so.)
